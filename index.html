Concept 2: The Snapshot

If the Sub-Account is the empty office, the Snapshot is the furniture, computers, and filing systems delivered in a single box.

A Snapshot is a pre-configured template. Instead of writing emails or designing web pages from scratch, we "load" a Snapshot that contains proven assets.

  • 1 Funnels: Pre-built websites to sell your service.
  • 2 Workflows: Automated email & SMS sequences.
  • 3 Pipelines: A visual board to track your leads.

How It Works

The 4-step deployment journey.

1
